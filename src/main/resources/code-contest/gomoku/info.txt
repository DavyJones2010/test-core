The rules:

1. What you need is to implement the AI part by implement five.player.Player. Check details from the source code in the jar.

2. We play the original game rule: five pieces at one line marks a win! No additional rules.

3. If no winner after the board is fulfilled, the 2nd player wins.

4. If the player give invalid step, or throws exception, or no return in 5 seconds. The player lose the game!

5. Each player play with every other player and match twice - as first player and second player respectively.

6. Winner got score. Loser got zero.

7. The rules here may subject to change later on in the final game.
